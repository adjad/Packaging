What we do

We used software to find packaging matierals that are both cost effective and safe to the enviroment.

After learning that packaging materials account for 30% of all waste in the US, we created Safe Packaging to prevent packaging material waste. We use software to find out the most enviromently safe packaging material(s)

The impact Safe Packaging will have

By Working with us, you will:

1. Save the Environment

2. Find cost effective materials for your package

3. Help small packaging suppliers stay afloat
