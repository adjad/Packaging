Safe Packaging

Save the environment

By using Safe Packaging, your company will be able to find the environmentally safe materials to put onto your package.

Our software uses your package's dimensions and size to find out the most environmentally safe way to package it and the most cost effective way to add packaging material

Help local package suppliers

This year has been tough on many package suppliers, and we hope to help them stay afloat with our product.

We are a non-profit organization that plans to reduce packaging material waste, which accounts for 30% of all waste in the US and help local packaging matieral suppliers .
